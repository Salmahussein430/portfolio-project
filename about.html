Frontend Developer | Based in the UK

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website. Im currently based in Birmingham, England studying Front-end development with SheCodes workshop-an introduction into coding for women via online lessons and projects.
Having some accounting and mathematics educational background, I had never thought about learning coding until recently. I currently work as a civil servant doing administration however coding and the tech industry has always fascinated me and with the lack of young female representation, i wanted to create change.
My love for coding has grown and creating new websites/apps has been very challenging yet rewarding. I plan on coding more projects in the future and look forward to working with other creatives to design awesome new experiences.
